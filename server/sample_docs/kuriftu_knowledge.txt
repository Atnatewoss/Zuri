# Welcome to Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu

## Overview
Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu is a premier destination in Ethiopia, located on the shores of Lake Bishoftu. We are famous for our overwater spa and the largest water park in East Africa.

## Accommodations
- Standard Room ($97/night): Cozy and comfortable for solo or couple travelers.
- Deluxe Room ($116/night): Spacious with premium lakeside views.
- Presidential Suite ($250/night): The ultimate luxury experience with private lounge.

## Services & Activities
- Spa: We offer Swedish massages, facials, and aromatherapy.
- Water Park: Features wave pools, slides, and family play areas.
- Dining: Three restaurants serving local and international cuisine.
