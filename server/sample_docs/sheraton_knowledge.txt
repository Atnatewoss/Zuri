# Sheraton Addis - The Luxury Collection

## The Hotel
Sheraton Addis is situated in the heart of Addis Ababa, offering unparalleled luxury and service since 1998. It is a member of The Luxury Collection.

## Elegant Rooms
- Classic King ($440/night): 323 sq ft of refined contemporary space.
- Executive Junior Suite ($650/night): Separate living area and premium amenities.
- Royal Villa ($2500/night): Private pool and dedicated butler service.

## World-Class Dining
We host 11 restaurants and bars, including:
- Stagioni: Fine Italian dining.
- Gaslight: The most exclusive nightclub in the city.
- Aqva Club: Refreshments by the pool.

## Health & Beauty
The Aqva Club features a health club, sauna, steam room, and heated outdoor pools.
