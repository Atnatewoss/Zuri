# Sheraton Addis - Luxury Data Profile

## [Knowledge Base]
### Luxury Collection Branding
The Sheraton Addis is a Luxury Collection Hotel, representing the pinnacle of refinement in Ethiopia. It is situated on a lush hilltop, offering sanctuary from the bustling city while remaining in the direct center of the diplomatic district.

### Elite Accommodations
- **Royal Villa:** The choice of visiting heads of state. Includes a private pool, security quarters, and dedicated 24-hour butler service. Rate: $2,500 - $5,000/night.
- **Junior Suite:** Contemporary design with African accents. Features a separate lounge area, marble bathroom, and premium "Luxury Collection" bedding. Rate: $800/night.
- **Executive King:** Designed for the high-end business traveler. High-performance workspace and ergonomic seating. Rate: $550/night.

### Epicurean Excellence (11 Outlets)
- **Stagioni:** Fine Italian dining with handmade pastas.
- **Summer Palace:** Authentic Cantonese flavors.
- **Les Ambassadeurs:** One of Africa's most respected French restaurants.
- **Gaslight Club:** The ultimate nightlife destination with high-end spirits and live DJs.

### Wellness (Aqva Club)
- **Pools:** Iconic outdoor heated pools with underwater music.
- **Spa:** Rejuvenating Aqva Spa treatments including deep tissue and aromatherapy.
- **Health Club:** Modern gym with personal training available.
