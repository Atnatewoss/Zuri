# Haile Resort Hawassa - Official Guest Handbook

## 1. The Haile Legend
Inspired by the legendary long-distance runner Haile Gebrselassie, Haile Resort Hawassa embodies the spirit of discipline and excellence. Situated on the shores of Lake Hawassa, we combine 4-star luxury with the warm hospitality of Southern Ethiopia.

## 2. Luxury Living

## 3. Gastronomy
- **African Heritage Restaurant:** Serving traditional spicy Dorowat and local Hawassa tilapia.
- **International Terrace:** Buffet breakfast and a wide selection of Western pastries and steaks.
- **The Beach Bar:** Relax by the sand with refreshing juices and local brews.
- **Room Service:** Available 24/7.

## 4. Eden Spa & Wellness
- **Full Range Treatments:** Swedish massage, reflexology, body scrubs, and facials.
- **Aquatic Life:** Year-round outdoor swimming pool and a dedicated children's pool with a waterslide.
- **Fitness:** Modern gym, sauna, and steam room.

## 5. Activities & Recreation
- **Mini-Golf:** Competitive course for all ages.
- **Horseback Riding:** Guided tours along the lakeside.
- **Kids Club:** Safe play areas with indoor and outdoor games.
- **Water Sports:** Boat trips on Lake Hawassa to see local hippos.

## 6. Business & Events
We feature 5 multi-purpose halls equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual tech, perfect for:
- Corporate workshops.
- Weddings (up to 500 guests).
- International conferences.
