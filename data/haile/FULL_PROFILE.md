# Haile Resort Hawassa - Complete Demo Profile

## [Knowledge Base]
### Introduction
Haile Resort Hawassa is part of the Haile Hotels & Resorts chain, owned by the world-famous athlete Haile Gebrselassie. Located on a 42,000 sqm plot along the shores of Lake Hawassa, it offers a "Home Away From Home" experience.

### Room Inventory
- **Presidential Suite (Lake Front):** Massive living quarters, dining area, three bathrooms, and uninterrupted lake views. Rate: $450/night.
- **One Bedroom Apartment:** Includes a living room, separate bedroom, and a fully equipped kitchenette (fridge, stove, microwave). Perfect for long-stay business travelers. Rate: $190/night.
- **Classic Queen:** High-quality queen size bed, garden view, desk, and satellite TV. Rate: $120/night.

### Services & Leisure
- **Swimming Pool:** Largest outdoor pool in Hawassa with a year-round temperature control and a children's section with a slide.
- **Eden Spa:** Professional wellness center. Services include massages, body scrubs, facials, and a dedicated beauty salon for hair and nails.
- **Mini-Golf & Horseriding:** Unique outdoor activities. Mini-golf is 200 ETB per session. Horseriding is guided along the lake shore.
- **Conferences:** 5 Halls (Haile Hall, Gebrselassie Hall, etc.) capacity from 50 to 500 people.

### Dining
- **Four Distinct Restaurants:** Serving Ethiopian classic, European, and specifically "Hawassa Tilapia" prepared in 5 different styles.
- **Morning Breakfast:** Complimentary for all staying guests (6:30 AM - 10:00 AM).
