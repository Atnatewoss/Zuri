### Overview
Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu is luxury redefined. Nestled on the volcanic crater lake of Bishoftu, we offer a unique blend of Ethiopian culture and world-class luxury only 45 minutes from Addis Ababa.

### Accommodation Details
- **Presidential Suite (Lake View):** 1,625 sq ft. Features a master bedroom with king bed, second bedroom with two twin beds, large living area, and private balcony overlooking the lake. Best for families or VVIPs. Rate: $350 - $450/night.
- **Junior Suite:** 757 sq ft. Cozy stone-walled room with a fireplace, king bed, and additional sofa bed. Ideal for couples seeking a romantic getaway. Rate: $210 - $280/night.
- **Premium Double/Twin:** 495 sq ft. Modern Ethiopian decor, private balcony, and state-of-the-art bathrooms. Rate: $150/night.

### Facilities & Services
- **Kuriftu Water Park:** The premier water destination in the region. Includes a massive wave pool, multiple corkscrew slides, a Boomerango slide, and a specifically designed "Funtown" for kids under 10. Open daily 10 AM to 6 PM.
- **The Overwater Spa:** Our signature facility. Treatment rooms are built on stilts over the water. Specialty: Swedish Massage, Hot Stone, and Deep Tissue. Reflexology is available for ETB 2,500.
- **Dining:**
  - **Main Lakeview Restaurant:** Serves both buffet and a la carte. Specializes in "Tere Siga" (raw beef) on Sundays.
  - **Floating Bar (18-36):** For the best sunset views in Bishoftu. Signature cocktail: The 'Bishoftu Bliss'.

### FAQ for AI Bot
- **Check-in/Out:** 2 PM / 12 PM. Late checkout available for gold members.
- **Airport Shuttle:** We provide a 4x4 Land Cruiser shuttle from Bole Airport. Cost: $35 per person.
- **Laundry:** Same-day service available if dropped off before 10 AM.
