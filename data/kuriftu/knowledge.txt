# Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu - Complete Guest Directory

## 1. Introduction
Kuriftu Resort & Spa Bishoftu is Ethiopia’s premier luxury retreat, located just 45 minutes from Addis Ababa on the scenic ridges of Lake Bishoftu. Known for its distinct architecture that blends Ethiopian heritage with modern comfort, the resort offers an unparalleled escape for relaxation and adventure.

## 3. Kuriftu Water Park
East Africa's largest water park is located right on our grounds!
- **Features:** Huge wave pool, extreme water houses, multi-lane slides, and children's play areas.
- **Operating Hours:** 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily.
- **Access:** Guests receive discounted entry or complimentary access depending on their room package.

## 4. Dining & Bars
- **The Lakeview Restaurant:** Our signature fine-dining outlet serving authentic Ethiopian "Tere Siga" and international steaks.
- **Pizzeria:** Wood-fired pizzas served in a casual outdoor setting.
- **Garden Bar:** Handcrafted cocktails and local St. George beer with live jazz on weekends.
- **The 18-36 Sunset Bar:** A stunning overwater bar perfect for sundowners.

## 5. Overwater Spa & Wellness

- **Swedish Massage:** 60/90 minutes ($60/$80).
